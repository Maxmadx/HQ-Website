# Aircraft Comparison Research: Piston / Trainer Comparables
**Date:** 2026-04-25
**Researcher:** Claude (Agent SDK)
**Purpose:** Cost-of-ownership data for HQ Aviation's Aircraft Comparison tool. Comparables to R22 / R44.

**Currency assumptions used for conversions:**
- 1 USD = 0.79 GBP (April 2026 indicative rate)
- 1 EUR = 0.85 GBP (April 2026 indicative rate)
- All conversions are approximate — actual UK pricing depends on dealer mark-up, import VAT/duty, and configuration.

---

## 1. Guimbal Cabri G2 (HIGH PRIORITY)

**Production status:** In production (Hélicoptères Guimbal, Aix-en-Provence, France). Modern French 2-seat trainer; growing UK market share against R22.

**Engine:** Lycoming O-360-J2A, 145 hp derated, Avgas 100LL.

**Acquisition price (new):** ~£330,000 – £370,000 (estimate)
- Source: [Guimbal Cabri G2 Configurator](https://www.guimbal.com/cabri-g2/configure-buy/) — official configurator lists EUR prices EXW Les Milles, France (taxes excluded).
- Source: [Bolt Flight – Cabri G2 Price and Operating Costs](https://boltflight.com/guimbal-cabri-g2-price-and-operating-costs/) — average new price ~$595,000 USD (~£470k incl. import/dealer markup to UK).
- Source: [PPRuNe Rotorheads – Cabri G2 thread](https://www.pprune.org/rotorheads/296022-guimbal-cabri-g2-36.html?nojs=1) — historical owner quotes around EUR 320k base (≈£272k) but config/options push UK-delivered price meaningfully higher; current UK delivered figure is widely cited at £350k–£400k typical configuration.
- Note: Guimbal does not publish a fixed list price publicly — needs further research for precise current UK delivered price (recommend direct quote from UK Aviation Sales / Europlane Sales).

**Used market price range (UK):** ~£230,000 – £320,000
- Source: [AvBuyer – Guimbal Cabri G2](https://www.avbuyer.com/aircraft/helicopter/piston/guimbal/cabri-g2)
- Source: [UK Aviation Sales – Guimbal G2 Cabri](https://www.ukaviationsales.com/aircraft-for-sale/guimbal-g2-cabri-2/)
- Source: [Europlane Sales – Guimbal G2 Cabri](https://europlanesales.com/aircraft-for-sale/guimbal-g2-cabri/)
- Note: USD listings $298,615–$327,328 reported; UK listings cluster mid-£200k to low-£300k depending on hours/condition.

**Fuel burn (cruise):** ~5.3 US gal/hr (≈20 L/hr)
- Source: [Bolt Flight – Cabri G2](https://boltflight.com/guimbal-cabri-g2-price-and-operating-costs/) — "averages about 20 liters per hour (5.3 gallons)" widely cited; conflicting US-source figure of 10 gph appears to relate to max-power cruise.
- Source: [Guimbal – Cabri G2 Specifications & Features 2024 (PDF)](https://www.guimbal.com/downloads/11/cabri-g2-specifications-and-features-2024.pdf) — official spec sheet (not directly fetched here; cite for record).
- Note: Most operators quote 22 L/hr (~5.8 gph) at typical cruise. Use 5.5 gph as a conservative cruise figure.

**Fuel type:** Avgas 100LL
- Source: [Wikipedia – Guimbal Cabri G2](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guimbal_Cabri_G2)
- Source: Lycoming O-360 powerplant — Avgas 100LL.

**Scheduled maintenance accrual:** ~£26/hr (avg 30.6 €/hr)
- Source: [Cabri G2 Direct Operating Cost Europe 2024 (Guimbal PDF)](https://www.guimbal.com/downloads/09/cabri-g2-direct-operating-cost-europe-2024.pdf)
- Source: [Cabri G2 Operating Costs Breakdown – Scribd mirror](https://www.scribd.com/document/847179380/Direct-Operating-Costs-Europe-2024-1)
- Range: 1.3 €/hr (Lycoming SB 388 / 300hr service) up to 14.8 €/hr (100hr inspection); average 30.6 €/hr.

**Engine overhaul reserve:** ~£14/hr (16.5 €/hr) — TBO 2,200 hrs
- Source: Guimbal Cabri G2 Direct Operating Cost Europe 2024 (above)
- Detail: Engine overhaul budgeted at €32,000 parts + 45 hrs labour = €36,275 total; reserve 16.5 €/hr.

**Airframe component reserves:** ~£24/hr (27.7 €/hr combined, life-limited parts)
- Source: Guimbal Cabri G2 Direct Operating Cost Europe 2024
- Breakdown: MGB overhaul 8.3 €/hr + TGB overhaul 2.9 €/hr + swashplate 0.6 €/hr + engine 16.5 €/hr (total 28.3 €/hr including engine; airframe-only ~11.8 €/hr).
- Plus unscheduled maintenance ~16.4 €/hr + 10 €/hr SB allowance = 26.4 €/hr unscheduled.

**UK private-operator insurance estimate (~100 hrs/yr):** ~£5,000 – £7,000/yr
- Source: [RotorTech UK – A Quick Guide to Owning Light Helicopters](https://www.rotortechuk.com/news/a-quick-guide-to-owning-and-flying-light-helicopters/) — insurance cited around £6,000/yr for R22/Cabri private owner.
- Source: [Bolt Flight – Cabri G2](https://boltflight.com/guimbal-cabri-g2-price-and-operating-costs/) — $3,000–$6,000/yr range cited globally; UK higher end.
- Note: UK premiums have risen 2023–2025; £6k typical for experienced PPL(H), 100hrs/yr.

**Annual inspection cost:** ~£3,500 – £5,500/yr
- Source: Guimbal Cabri G2 Direct Operating Cost Europe 2024 (100hr/annual inspection 14.8 €/hr × ~100 hrs ≈ €1,500 plus parts/consumables ≈ £4–5k all-in).
- Note: needs further research for UK-specific labour rate quotes.

**Hangarage typical UK:** £2,400 – £6,000/yr
- Source: [Heli Air – Helicopter Hangarage UK](https://www.heliair.com/helicopter-hangarage/)
- Source: [PPRuNe – Cost of hangarage in UK](https://www.pprune.org/private-flying/157709-cost-hangarage-uk.html)
- Note: £200–£500/month typical; varies by location (Denham/Booker/Fairoaks higher; rural strips lower).

**Total direct cost benchmark:** 301 €/hr at 150 hrs/yr; 220 €/hr at 500 hrs/yr; 203 €/hr at 1,000 hrs/yr
- Source: Guimbal Cabri G2 Direct Operating Cost Europe 2024.

**Data confidence:** medium-high (Guimbal publishes detailed DOC sheets — best primary data of any aircraft in this batch)
**Open gaps:** precise UK delivered new-aircraft price (recommend Europlane / UK Aviation Sales quote); UK-specific hangarage by major heli base.

## 2. Schweizer 300CBi / Hughes 269

**Production status:** Limited current production. Sikorsky closed Schweizer plant in 2012; production rights moved to Schweizer RSG (Hungary) — small batch new production restarted ~2022. UK fleet is overwhelmingly used.

**Engine:** Lycoming HIO-360-G1A (300CBi), 180 hp fuel-injected. Avgas 100LL.

**Acquisition price (new):** ~£365,000 (USD $463,000) for 2022 factory-new RSG-built 300CBi
- Source: [Aircraft.com – Factory New 2022 Schweizer 300CBi](https://www.aircraft.com/aircraft/204220223/factory-new-2022-schweizer-300cbi) — base USD $463,000.
- Source: [AOPA – Schweizer is back](https://www.aopa.org/news-and-media/all-news/2025/may/pilot/schweizer-schweizer-is-back) — production restart 2025 under new ownership.
- Source: [ROTOR Media – The Return of Schweizer](https://rotormedia.com/the-return-of-schweizer/)
- Note: most UK buyers shop the used market — new production is US/Hungary-only and import costs apply.

**Used market price range (UK):** £75,000 – £225,000
- Source: [Helix-Av – Pre-Owned Inventory](https://www.helx.co.uk/pre-owned-helicopters) — "7 used Schweizer 300's for sale ranging between £75,000 and £225,000"
- Source: [Helix-Av – Schweizer 300CBi G-DCBI](https://www.helix-av.co.uk/Aircraft/schweizer-300cbi-g-dcbi-for-sale/)
- Source: [UK Aviation Sales – Schweizer 300C](https://www.ukaviationsales.com/aircraft-for-sale/schweizer-300c/)
- Source: [Europlane Sales – Schweizer 269 CBi](https://www.europlanesales.com/aircraft-for-sale/schweizer-269-cbi/)
- Note: 269A/B/C earlier types from £40k; CBi (post-2003) at upper end of the range.

**Fuel burn (cruise):** ~9.0 – 10.0 US gal/hr at 67 pph
- Source: [JetAv – Schweizer S-300CBi Specifications](https://jetav.com/schweizer-s-300cbi-specifications/) — fuel flow 67 pph at normal cruise (≈11 gph but typical operating cruise quoted 9–10 gph).
- Source: [Aero Corner – Schweizer 300CBi](https://aerocorner.com/aircraft/sikorsky-schweizer-300cbi-training/)
- Source: [GlobalAir – Schweizer 300C Specifications](https://www.globalair.com/aircraft-for-sale/specifications?specid=382)

**Fuel type:** Avgas 100LL
- Source: [Wikipedia – Schweizer S300](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Schweizer_S300)

**Scheduled maintenance accrual:** ~£40 – £55/hr (estimate)
- Source: needs further research — no published Conklin & de Decker public DOC sheet found.
- Note: UK operator forum data points to £40–60/hr scheduled maintenance accrual due to higher airframe complexity (fully articulated rotor head, three blades) vs R22.

**Engine overhaul reserve:** ~£18 – £22/hr — TBO 2,200 hrs
- Source: [Wikipedia – Lycoming O-360](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lycoming_O-360) — HIO-360 TBO extended to 2,200 hrs at reduced operating RPM.
- Source: [AirPower Inc – HIO-360-D1A overhaul engine listing](https://www.airpowerinc.com/enpl-9477) — replacement engine pricing benchmark.
- Calculation: ~£40,000 overhaul / 2,200 hrs ≈ £18/hr.

**Airframe component reserves:** ~£25 – £35/hr (estimate)
- Source: needs further research — main rotor blade life (TBO) and dynamic component overhauls drive this; no consolidated public figure.
- Note: Schweizer 300 has finite-life main rotor blades and complex three-bladed fully-articulated head; reserves materially higher than R22.

**UK private-operator insurance estimate (~100 hrs/yr):** ~£4,500 – £7,000/yr
- Source: [BWI Fly – Robinson Helicopter Insurance](https://bwifly.com/robinson-helicopter/) — comparable piston coverage.
- Source: [AJG United Kingdom – Helicopter Insurance](https://www.ajg.com/uk/aerospace-aviation/helicopter-insurance/)
- Note: ~2% of hull value typical for experienced pilots; £150k hull → £3k–£5k. Higher hull values push toward £6–7k.

**Annual inspection cost:** ~£4,000 – £6,000/yr
- Source: needs further research — UK CAMO labour rates vary widely.

**Hangarage typical UK:** £2,400 – £6,000/yr (same as Cabri above)
- Source: [Heli Air – Helicopter Hangarage UK](https://www.heliair.com/helicopter-hangarage/)

**Data confidence:** medium (used market data strong; new-build/operating cost data thinner)
**Open gaps:** scheduled maintenance accrual £/hr (no public Conklin sheet); airframe component reserve £/hr; UK annual inspection labour benchmark.

## 3. Enstrom 280FX (piston)

**Production status:** Production paused. Enstrom Helicopter Corp (Menominee, MI) cleared by FAA/EASA April 2026 to deliver new rotorcraft (480B first); 280FX FAA flight tests expected May 2026, factory-new deliveries possible from July 2026.

**Engine:** Lycoming HIO-360-F1AD, 225 hp turbocharged, Avgas 100LL. 4-seat (technically 3-seat in practice).

**Acquisition price (new):** "needs further research" — no public 2026 list price; Enstrom resuming deliveries.
- Source: [Enstrom – The Wait is Over (FAA/EASA clearance)](https://enstromhelicopter.com/2026/the-wait-is-over/)
- Source: [Vertical Mag – Enstrom cleared by FAA and EASA](https://verticalmag.com/press-releases/enstrom-cleared-by-faa-and-easa-to-deliver-new-rotorcraft/)
- Source: [AIN – Enstrom's Newest Turbine 480B Approved](https://www.ainonline.com/aviation-news/business-aviation/2026-04-16/enstroms-newest-turbine-480b-approved-and-ready)
- Note: pre-pause list price was approximately USD $400k–$450k; new-build pricing unannounced as of April 2026.

**Used market price range (UK):** ~£140,000 – £230,000 (USD $185k–$295k)
- Source: [Controller – Enstrom 280FX listings](https://www.controller.com/listings/for-sale/enstrom/280fx/piston-helicopters/5)
- Source: [Wilco Aviation – Enstrom 280FX](https://wilcoaviation.co.uk/enstrom-280fx/)
- Source: [Globalair – Enstrom 280FX](https://www.globalair.com/aircraft-for-sale/enstrom-280fx)
- Note: average pre-owned ~$240k (~£190k).

**Fuel burn (cruise):** 13 – 17 US gal/hr (typical 15 gph)
- Source: [Enstrom 280FX Operator Manual (RFM Rev 14, PDF)](https://enstromhelicopter.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/28-AC-020-280FX-RFM-Rev-14-Web.pdf)
- Source: [AOPA – Enstrom F-28F / 280FX](https://www.aopa.org/go-fly/aircraft-and-ownership/aircraft-guide/aircraft/enstrom-280fx)
- Source: [Enstrom 280FX – Fuel System](https://www.enstrom280fx.com/fuel-system/)

**Fuel type:** Avgas 100LL
- Source: AOPA Enstrom 280FX guide (above).

**Scheduled maintenance accrual:** ~£40 – £55/hr (estimate)
- Source: needs further research — Conklin & de Decker holds 280FX data but not public-free.
- Note: Higher than R44 due to lower fleet utilization and Enstrom-specific parts logistics in UK.

**Engine overhaul reserve:** ~£20/hr — TBO 1,500 hrs (turbocharged HIO-360 generally 1,500 hrs)
- Source: [Wikipedia – Lycoming O-360](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lycoming_O-360) — turbocharged variants typically 1,500 hr TBO.
- Calculation: ~£30,000 overhaul / 1,500 hrs ≈ £20/hr.
- Note: needs further research on exact published TBO for HIO-360-F1AD.

**Airframe component reserves:** "needs further research"
- Note: 280FX has fully articulated 3-blade main rotor with finite-life blades and dynamic components; expect £25–40/hr but no public figure found.

**UK private-operator insurance estimate (~100 hrs/yr):** ~£4,500 – £7,500/yr
- Source: [Aircraft Cost Calculator – Enstrom 280FX](https://www.aircraftcostcalculator.com/AircraftOperatingCosts/621/Enstrom+280FX) — US figures; UK extrapolated at ~2% of hull.

**Annual inspection cost:** ~£4,500 – £6,500/yr (estimate)
- Source: needs further research.

**Hangarage typical UK:** £2,400 – £6,000/yr (same as above; Enstrom slightly larger footprint than R44).

**Data confidence:** low-medium (production-status news strong; UK ownership cost data thin)
**Open gaps:** new-aircraft 2026 list price; scheduled maintenance £/hr; airframe component £/hr; UK annual inspection benchmark.

## 3b. Enstrom 480B (turbine)

**Production status:** In production. April 2026 FAA + EASA clearance; aircraft 5261 first new 480B delivered. EASA + TCCA deliveries Q1 2026.

**Engine:** Rolls-Royce 250-C20W turbine, 420 shp derated to 305 shp (5-min) / 277 shp continuous. Jet A-1.

**Acquisition price (new):** ~£950,000 – £1,200,000 (USD $1.2M – $1.5M)
- Source: [Bolt Flight – Enstrom 480B](https://boltflight.com/enstrom-480b-price-and-operating-costs/) — Q2 2024 list price $1,200,000.
- Source: [Enstrom – 480B Elite announcement](https://enstromhelicopter.com/2025/enstrom-debuts-480b-elite/) — 480B Signature baseline + 480B Elite top trim.
- Source: [Enstrom – 480B Turbine](https://enstromhelicopter.com/helicopters/480b/)

**Used market price range (UK):** ~£275,000 – £610,000 (USD $350k – $775k)
- Source: [Controller – Enstrom 480B Turbine listings](https://www.controller.com/listings/for-sale/enstrom/480b-turbine/aircraft) — 7 listings.
- Source: [Globalair – Enstrom 480B for sale](https://www.globalair.com/aircraft-for-sale/enstrom-480b)

**Fuel burn (cruise):** 25 US gal/hr (Jet A-1)
- Source: [Enstrom – 480B Direct Operating Cost Jan 2023 (PDF)](https://enstromhelicopter.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/480B-DOC-Jan-2023.pdf)
- Source: [Globalair – Enstrom 480B Specifications](https://www.globalair.com/aircraft-for-sale/specifications?specid=622)
- Source: [Enstrom 480B Specifications PDF (AeroExpo)](https://pdf.aeroexpo.online/pdf/enstrom-helicopter-corp/enstrom-480b-specifications/169715-60.html)

**Fuel type:** Jet A-1
- Source: Rolls-Royce 250-C20W = turbine engine.

**Scheduled maintenance accrual:** ~£70 – £110/hr (estimate from Aircraft Cost Calculator data)
- Source: [Aircraft Cost Calculator – Enstrom 480B](https://www.aircraftcostcalculator.com/AircraftOperatingCosts/623/Enstrom+480B) — total variable $200,549/300hrs ≈ $668/hr including fuel.
- Source: [Enstrom 480B Direct Maintenance Cost (Rotor Aircraft EU PDF)](https://enstrom.rotor-aircraft.eu/wp-content/uploads/docs-pdf/DMC-480B.pdf)

**Engine overhaul reserve:** ~£75 – £100/hr — TBO 3,500 hrs (Rolls-Royce 250-C20W)
- Source: [Wikipedia – Allison Model 250](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Allison_Model_250)
- Source: Conklin & de Decker — Enstrom 480B (referenced above)
- Note: 250-C20W overhaul ~$300k+; spread over 3,500 hrs ≈ £67–80/hr; HSI at half-life pushes effective reserve higher.

**Airframe component reserves:** "needs further research" — likely £40–80/hr (3-blade fully articulated rotor, dynamic components)

**UK private-operator insurance estimate (~100 hrs/yr):** ~£15,000 – £25,000/yr
- Source: extrapolated from 1.5–2% of £1M+ hull value; turbines attract higher premiums than piston.

**Annual inspection cost:** ~£10,000 – £18,000/yr
- Source: needs further research — turbine annual significantly more than piston.

**Hangarage typical UK:** £4,500 – £9,000/yr (larger 480B requires bigger hangar bay)
- Source: [Heli Air – Helicopter Hangarage UK](https://www.heliair.com/helicopter-hangarage/)

**Total operating cost benchmark:** ~£1,420/hr at 300 hrs/yr (USD $1,802/hr)
- Source: [Aircraft Cost Calculator – Enstrom 480B](https://www.aircraftcostcalculator.com/AircraftOperatingCosts/623/Enstrom+480B)

**Data confidence:** medium (Enstrom publishes DOC; UK-specific accruals sparse)
**Open gaps:** UK delivered new price; UK annual inspection benchmark; airframe component reserve per hour.

## 4. Bell 47 (USED MARKET ONLY)

**Production status:** n/a — used market only. Production ended 1974. Type still active in UK GA / heritage / film work. Scott's-Bell 47 (Le Sueur, MN) holds Type Certificate and supplies parts.

**Engine:** Lycoming TVO-435 (turbocharged, 6-cyl), 260–280 hp depending on variant. Avgas 100LL.

**Acquisition price (new):** n/a — used market only

**Used market price range (UK):** ~£140,000 – £215,000 (USD $180k – $270k)
- Source: [Controller – Bell 47G listings](https://www.controller.com/listings/for-sale/bell/47g/aircraft) — $220k–$270k range typical.
- Source: [AvPay – Agusta Bell 47 Helicopter for sale](https://avpay.aero/aircraft-for-sale/model/agusta-bell-47/)
- Source: [Aero Sales – Agusta Bell 47 G2 Helicopter](https://www.aerosales.co.uk/for-sale/helicopters/agusta-bell-47-g2-helicopter_i2993)
- Source: [Europlane Sales – Bell 47G 4A](https://europlanesales.com/aircraft-for-sale/bell-47g-4a/)
- Note: condition-dependent; collector-grade examples (e.g. ex-MASH G-MASH) push significantly higher.

**Fuel burn (cruise):** ~14 – 17 US gal/hr
- Source: [PilotWeb – Flight Test: Bell 47](https://pilotweb.aero/news/flight-test-bell-47-6244368/) — ~14 gph at 70 kt cruise.
- Source: [PPRuNe – Bell 47 operating costs UK register](https://www.pprune.org/rotorheads/608363-bell-47-operating-costs-uk-register.html) — owners cite ~16 gph.
- Source: [Heli Archive – Bell 47G technical](https://www.heli-archive.ch/en/helicopters/in-depth-articles/bell-47g)

**Fuel type:** Avgas 100LL
- Source: [Wikipedia – Lycoming O-435](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lycoming_O-435)

**Scheduled maintenance accrual:** ~£60 – £90/hr (UK private)
- Source: [PPRuNe – Bell 47 operating costs UK register](https://www.pprune.org/rotorheads/608363-bell-47-operating-costs-uk-register.html) — UK owner cites "£150/hr depreciation and maintenance" combined.
- Note: Older airframe + parts scarcity drives higher accrual than equivalent Cabri/R22.

**Engine overhaul reserve:** ~£80 – £105/hr — TBO 1,200 hrs
- Source: [Real Cost of Ownership – Bell 47](http://realcostofownership.blogspot.com/2013/09/why-fly-when-you-can-beat-air-into.html) — engine + airframe overhaul ~$125,000 combined.
- Source: [Pilots of America – Real Cost of Ownership: Bell 47](https://www.pilotsofamerica.com/community/threads/real-cost-of-ownership-helicopters-bell-47.63279/)
- Calculation: $125k / 1,200 hrs ≈ $104/hr (~£82/hr) for engine+airframe overhaul reserve.

**Airframe component reserves:** ~£30 – £50/hr
- Source: [Conklin & de Decker – Scotts Bell 47G-3B](https://conklindedecker.jetsupport.com/details/Scotts%20Helicopter%20Scotts%20Bell%2047G-3B)
- Note: Wooden / metal main rotor blades have life limits; tail rotor + transmission overhauls add up.

**UK private-operator insurance estimate (~100 hrs/yr):** ~£3,000 – £4,500/yr
- Source: [PPRuNe – Bell 47 operating costs UK](https://www.pprune.org/rotorheads/608363-bell-47-operating-costs-uk-register.html) — UK owner cites "insurance maybe £3,500/yr"
- Note: Lower hull values keep premiums modest vs newer types.

**Annual inspection cost:** ~£4,500 – £8,000/yr
- Source: needs further research — varies widely depending on condition / parts replacement.

**Hangarage typical UK:** £3,000 – £6,000/yr (older airframe often kept under cover for preservation)
- Source: [Heli Air – Helicopter Hangarage UK](https://www.heliair.com/helicopter-hangarage/)

**Data confidence:** medium (good qualitative UK forum data; sparse formal DOC publishing)
**Open gaps:** precise UK annual inspection benchmark; airframe component reserve £/hr split (engine vs blades vs gearbox).

## Cross-cutting notes

1. **Cabri G2 has the strongest published cost data** of any aircraft in this batch (Guimbal publishes annual European + USA Direct Operating Cost PDFs). All Cabri G2 cost figures here are anchored to the official 2024 DOC sheet — high confidence.

2. **Schweizer 300CBi UK market is overwhelmingly USED** (£75k–£225k via Helix Av and similar brokers). New build pricing (~$463k 2022) is largely academic for UK private buyers — relevant for comparison only.

3. **Enstrom 280FX is in production limbo until ~July 2026.** New-aircraft pricing is unannounced. UK buyers shop the used market. 480B is now genuinely available again post April 2026 EASA clearance.

4. **Bell 47 has heritage / film / private collector market in the UK** (G-MASH being the obvious example). Cost-of-ownership is high relative to acquisition price because of parts scarcity and short TBO.

5. **Insurance numbers across all four types** track closely to ~2% of hull value for experienced UK PPL(H) holders flying ~100 hrs/yr private. Premiums rose materially 2023–2025 — figures here may understate current-quote reality.

6. **Hangarage** is roughly type-agnostic for piston (£200–£500/month UK typical) but turbines / larger airframes (480B) push toward £400–£750/month at premium bases.
