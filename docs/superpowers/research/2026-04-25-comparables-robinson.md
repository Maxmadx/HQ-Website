# Robinson Helicopter Family — Cost-of-Ownership Research

**Research date:** 2026-04-25
**Researcher:** Claude (for HQ Aviation Aircraft Comparison tool)
**FX rate used:** 1 USD = 0.745 GBP (April 2026 average per Pound Sterling Live / EconomyPulse / exchange-rates.org). Where USD figures are converted, the rate and source date are noted.

> **STANDING CAVEAT (applies to every Robinson figure below):** HQ already maintains Robinsons internally and runs them on UK fuel/UK MX rates. Verify every per-hour cost number against HQ's MX records and operator P&Ls before seeding the comparison tool. The Robinson factory Estimated Operating Cost (EOC) sheets assume US labour rates, US fuel, US insurance and 500 flight hours/yr — NONE of which match a UK private-operator profile flying ~100 hrs/yr.

> **Inflation flag:** Robinson published a 5% factory price increase effective 1 January 2026 (ref: R44 Cadet news, Heli Air). Where the most recent firm number we could find is the 1 July 2025 price list, the 2026 number is +5% unless we have a 2026 sheet.

## R22 Beta II

**Acquisition price (new):** ~£279,000 (≈$375,000 USD)
- Source: Robinson 2025 price list as quoted by Quantum Helicopters and aircraftforsale.com listings (https://www.quantumhelicopters.com/2025-robinson-r22-beta-ii/, https://aircraftforsale.com/l/659cd5cc-47a2-4ace-910b-26391299c243). Robinson's official 1 July 2025 price list PDF is at https://robinsonstrapistorprod.blob.core.windows.net/uploads/assets/rhc_r22_pricelist_1_july_2025_2d4ce837c6.pdf (PDF could not be parsed directly — base figure cross-referenced via dealer sites).
- Note: $375,000 USD base × 0.745 = £279,375. Add UK delivery, optional avionics, paint, and 20% VAT for a private buyer = realistic UK on-the-ramp ~£340k–£365k inc. VAT. 2026 price is +5% per the announced January 2026 factory price rise (verify HQ's actual current quote sheet).

**Used market range:** ~£120,000 – £230,000 (5–10 yr old, depending on hours-since-overhaul and time remaining to 12-yr/2,200-hr)
- Source: Heli Air UK listings (https://www.heliair.com/product-category/pre-owned-and-used-helicopters/used-robinson-r22/), AvPay 2018 R22 Beta listing (https://avpay.aero/company/egbj-heliair-gloucester/product/2018-robinson-r22-beta-i-helicopter-for-sale/), UK Aviation Sales (https://www.ukaviationsales.com/aircraft-for-sale/robinson-r22-beta/), Controller EMEA UK listings (https://www.controlleremea.co.uk/listings/for-sale/robinson/piston-helicopters/5).
- Sample listings seen: 2018 R22 Beta with 700 hrs since overhaul listed at £165,000 + VAT (Heli Air, ex-2018); R22 Beta II with 700 hrs since factory new listed at £225,000 ex VAT. Global Controller listings span $33k (basket case, runout) to $392k (factory-fresh).
- Note: Used R22 pricing is brutal at the bottom end of TBO and very strong near zero-time post-overhaul. The 12-year/2,200-hr Robinson rebuild mandate is the dominant variable — a 2,100-hr/11.5-yr R22 is effectively only worth its rebuild kit + airframe.

**Fuel burn (cruise):** 7–10 US gph; ~9.4 gph at 75% power
- Source: Operator data summarised at AOPA (https://www.aopa.org/go-fly/aircraft-and-ownership/aircraft-guide/aircraft-guide-robinson-r22-beta-ii), Wikipedia (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robinson_R22), PPRuNe operator threads (https://www.pprune.org/rotorheads/229510-robinson-r22-corner-archive-copy-37.html), planephd.com (https://planephd.com/wizard/details/1328/ROBINSON-HELICOPTER-CO-R22-Beta-II-specifications-performance-operating-cost-valuation).
- Note: Operators report ~8–9 gph for standard cruise at 20" MAP, 10 gph in hover/manoeuvring. Use **8.5 gph** as a sensible mid-point for the comparison tool. POH does not publish a single fuel-flow figure.

**Fuel type:** Avgas 100LL
- Source: Robinson POH (https://robinsonstrapistorprod.blob.core.windows.net/uploads/assets/r22_poh_2_a730a7b2f8.pdf), Wikipedia.

**Scheduled maintenance accrual:** ~£25–£40/hr (verify against HQ MX records before seeding)
- Source: myaircraftcost.com (https://myaircraftcost.com/robinson-r22-operating-costs-a-comprehensive-breakdown/) cites $6–$12/hr direct MX in the US — that is unrealistically low for UK Part-145 rates. Aviation Consumer's R22 used-aircraft guide (https://aviationconsumer.com/used-aircraft-guide/used-aircraft-guide-robinson-r22/) is more realistic. UK MX labour at HQ-style rates of ~£100/hr quickly pushes 100-hr MX accrual into the £25–£40/hr range. **Needs HQ input** to confirm.

**Engine overhaul reserve:** ~£70–£75/hr (factory $90.42/hr at current rate)
- Source: aircraftcostcalculator.com (https://www.aircraftcostcalculator.com/AircraftOperatingCosts/633/Robinson+R22+Beta+II), Robinson factory EOC documents (https://robinsonstrapistorprod.blob.core.windows.net/uploads/assets/r22_eoc_july_2025_4712177127.pdf — could not parse PDF directly), Bladeslapper R22 overhaul thread (https://bladeslapper.com/viewtopic.php?t=4369). Robinson factory 2200-hr overhaul price list April 2024 (https://www.robinsonheli.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/r22-2200-factory-overhaul-april-2024.pdf).
- Note: Robinson factory's "reserve for overhaul" was $90.42/hr in mid-2024. At 0.745 GBP that's ~£67/hr. Operator-reported real overhauls run $100k–$120k all-in, which spreads to ~£35–£45/hr if you separate out the airframe component overhaul (below). The factory $90.42/hr is a *combined* engine + airframe figure for the 2,200-hr rebuild. **Verify with HQ.**

**Airframe component reserves:** combined into the 2,200-hr overhaul reserve above on the R22; not a separate line on Robinson's EOC.
- Source: same as above. Robinson's published reserve folds engine + airframe parts kit together because the Robinson 12-year/2,200-hr rebuild is one event.
- Note: For the comparison tool, present as a single "Robinson 2,200-hr / 12-yr rebuild reserve" line. **Needs HQ input** for an HQ-realistic split if buyers ask.

**UK private-operator insurance estimate:** £3,500 – £6,000/yr (hull + liability, ~100 hrs/yr private, qualified pilot)
- Source: BWI Fly (https://bwifly.com/blog/how-to-lower-your-robinson-r22-insurance-premium/) — US comparable; Pathfinder Indemnity (https://www.pathfinderindemnity.com/), Hayward Aviation, Horner Blakey (https://www.hornerblakey.co.uk/helicopter-insurance-everything-you-need-to-know/) — UK brokers. Robinson factory EOC quotes hull $7,496 + liability $1,920 (US). UK rates run lower for low-hull older R22s, higher for time-low pilots.
- Note: Range varies massively with pilot hours, hull value and named-pilot endorsements. **Needs HQ input** for a current UK quote band — HQ likely has live broker numbers from existing customers.

**Annual inspection cost:** ~£900 – £1,500/yr (labour only, parts/AD compliance extra)
- Source: Heli Aviation (https://heliaf.com/maintenance/) US base price $900 labour; UK Part-145 labour rates push this into £900–£1,500 territory. CAA private-CofA renewal admin is small (~£100s).
- Note: This excludes ADs, R/HUMS, life-limited parts. UK Part-M / Part-CAO continuing-airworthiness fees add a few hundred £/yr. **Needs HQ input.**

**Hangarage (UK):** Varies hugely; often £0 if home-based. Typical paid hangarage £3,000 – £8,000/yr at UK GA airfields (Denham, Elstree, Wycombe, Sywell, etc.).
- Source: ICE Helicopters Elstree long-term hangarage page (https://www.icehelicopters.com/long-term-overnight-helicopter-hangarage-london-elstree-aerodrome/), Heli Air UK hangarage page (https://www.heliair.com/helicopter-hangarage/), HQ Aviation Denham (https://hqaviation.com/), ukga.com Elstree (https://ukga.com/airfield/elstree/47552).
- Note: Specific monthly rates not publicly listed by ICE/Heli Air — they're quote-on-application. Public sources do not give a clean GBP/yr figure. **Flag for HQ to fill in based on what they charge / observe.**

**Data confidence:** partial
**Open gaps:** UK-specific MX accrual £/hr (HQ has the data); UK insurance for low-time PPL(H); UK hangarage typical band; CAA admin/airworthiness fees.

- Note: A recently-rebuilt mid-life R44 frequently sells *higher* than a low-hours younger R44 because the 12-yr clock has been reset. Used R44 pricing is highly individual.

**Fuel burn (cruise):** ~14–15 US gph at 75% power; ~10 gph at slow cruise
- Source: AOPA (https://www.aopa.org/go-fly/aircraft-and-ownership/aircraft-guide/aircraft/robinson-r44-raven-ii), Vertical Reference operator forum (https://helicopterforum.verticalreference.com/topic/564-r44-average-fuel-burn-per-hour/), Plane & Pilot 2008 review (https://planeandpilotmag.com/2008-robinson-r44-raven-ii/), Robinson EOC documents.
- Note: Robinson factory uses **15.0 gph** in EOC. Operators report 14–17 gph in hover, 12–14 in econ cruise. Use **15 gph** as the comparison-tool figure.

**Fuel type:** Avgas 100LL
- Source: AOPA, Robinson POH/website (https://www.robinsonheli.com/helicopters/r44).

**Scheduled maintenance accrual:** ~£40–£60/hr (verify against HQ MX records)
- Source: BWI Fly R44 operating-cost page (https://bwifly.com/robinson-r44-operating-cost/), aircraftloancalculator (https://aircraftloancalculator.com/articles/the-true-operating-cost-of-robinson-r44.html), Bolt Flight (https://boltflight.com/robinson-r44-raven-ii-price-and-operating-costs/). Annual MX/inspection cited at $5k–$10k/yr in US.
- Note: At 100 hrs/yr UK, the routine MX accrual probably lands £40–£60/hr at UK Part-145 labour rates. **Needs HQ input.**

**Engine overhaul reserve:** ~£31/hr (Robinson factory 2026 EOC: $41.77/hr engine alone)
- Source: Robinson R44 II 2026 EOC (https://robinsonstrapistorprod.blob.core.windows.net/uploads/assets/r44_raven_2_eoc_2026_3a2b4f3c1c.pdf — found via search, not parsed directly), summarised in search results: 2,200-hr engine overhaul ($91,900 RHC exchange) = $41.77/hr.
- Note: $41.77 × 0.745 = £31.12/hr. **Verify against HQ — they know the actual UK exchange engine cost.**

**Airframe component reserves:** ~£80/hr (Robinson factory: $94.18/hr parts kit + $13.30/hr labour = $107.48/hr)
- Source: same Robinson 2026 EOC. 2,200-hr aircraft overhaul parts kit $207,200 = $94.18/hr; labour 225 manhours × $130 = $13.30/hr.
- Note: $107.48 × 0.745 = £80.07/hr. **Total Robinson 2,200-hr reserve = $149.25/hr ≈ £111/hr** (engine + airframe combined). UK labour at £100–£130/hr is similar; the parts kit is dollar-priced so FX risk is real. **Verify with HQ.**

**UK private-operator insurance estimate:** £5,000 – £20,000/yr (hull + liability, varies massively with pilot hours, hull value, cover type)
- Source: BWI Fly (https://bwifly.com/robinson-r44-insurance/), Sunset Aviation Insurance (https://sunsetais.com/helicopters/robinson/), Horner Blakey UK (https://www.hornerblakey.co.uk/helicopter-insurance-everything-you-need-to-know/), Hayward Aviation. UK private-owner range £5k–£20k cited by Horner Blakey.
- Note: Mid-band ~£7,000–£10,000/yr for an experienced private owner with mid-hours hull (~£300–£400k). **Needs HQ input** for current broker quotes.

**Annual inspection cost:** ~£1,100 – £2,000/yr (labour only)
- Source: Heli Aviation US base $1,100 (https://heliaf.com/maintenance/). UK Part-145 rates push slightly higher.
- Note: Excludes ADs, life-limited part replacement, unscheduled findings. **Needs HQ input.**

**Hangarage (UK):** Varies hugely; often £0 if home-based. Typical £4,000–£10,000/yr at GA airfields with hangared helicopter parking.
- Source: ICE Helicopters Elstree (https://www.icehelicopters.com/long-term-overnight-helicopter-hangarage-london-elstree-aerodrome/), Heli Air (https://www.heliair.com/helicopter-hangarage/), HQ Aviation Denham (https://hqaviation.com/).
- Note: Public rate cards not published; quote-on-application. **Flag for HQ.**

**Data confidence:** partial — strong on factory reserves, weak on UK-specific MX/insurance/hangarage
**Open gaps:** UK MX £/hr at HQ rates; current UK insurance band; UK hangarage typical figure; whether HQ recommends component-by-component reserve breakouts.
